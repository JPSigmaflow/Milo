# Professional Edit Plan V2 - Women's Event (30s)

## Music Target: 120 BPM (0.5s per beat)
All cuts will be on beat markers for any 120 BPM track

## Structure (Dynamic Pacing):

### HOOK (0-5s) - Grab attention IMMEDIATELY
- 0-2s: BEST table moment (Frame 3 style - faces, laughter) 
- 2-3.5s: Quick cut to speaker (establish authority)
- 3.5-5s: Back to intimate conversation (2 women close-up)

### BUILD (5-15s) - Show diversity & energy  
Fast cuts, 1-2s each:
- 5-6.5s: Wide shot of venue (context)
- 6.5-8s: Hands gesturing in conversation
- 8-10s: Another laugh/smile moment
- 10-11.5s: Food/flowers detail (beauty shot)
- 11.5-13s: Group of 3-4 women talking
- 13-15s: Someone nodding/reacting

### PEAK (15-23s) - Emotional connection
Slower holds, 2-3s each:
- 15-18s: BEST emotional moment (genuine laugh/connection)
- 18-21s: Multi-generational shot (diversity of ages)
- 21-23s: Speaker moment (if powerful) OR group embrace

### RESOLUTION (23-30s) - Leave them inspired
- 23-27s: Wide establishing shot showing full community
- 27-30s: Fade to black with slight slow-mo on last frame

## Technical:
- All transitions: Hard cuts OR 0.2s cross-dissolve on emotional beats
- Color: Warm grade (+10 saturation, +5 warmth)
- Slight zoom-in (103% scale) on hero moments for energy
- NO long static shots - even "holds" have subtle movement
