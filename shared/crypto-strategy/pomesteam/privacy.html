🐾 PomesTeam — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

PomesTeam ("we", "our", "us") operates a content management platform for pet influencer communities. This Privacy Policy explains how PomesTeam collects, uses, and protects information when you use our application and services.

1. Information We Collect

When you use PomesTeam, we may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: When you connect your TikTok, Instagram, or Facebook account, we receive your public profile information (username, profile picture, account ID) as authorized by you through the platform's login process.
  • Content Data: Photos, videos, captions, and hashtags that you choose to publish through PomesTeam.
  • Analytics Data: Engagement metrics, follower counts, and content performance data from your connected social media accounts.
  • Usage Data: Basic interaction data with the PomesTeam platform (features used, pages visited).

2. How We Use Your Information

PomesTeam uses your information solely for the following purposes:

  • Publishing content to your authorized social media accounts (TikTok, Instagram, Facebook)
  • Displaying analytics and performance metrics for your content
  • Managing comments and community engagement on your behalf
  • Improving the PomesTeam platform and user experience

3. TikTok Data Usage

When you connect your TikTok account to PomesTeam:

  • We use Login Kit to authenticate your identity securely
  • We use the Content Posting API to publish videos to your TikTok account on your behalf
  • We do NOT access your private messages, contacts, or any data beyond what you explicitly authorize
  • We do NOT sell, share, or transfer your TikTok data to third parties
  • You can revoke PomesTeam's access at any time through your TikTok account settings

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Authentication tokens are stored securely and encrypted
  • We retain your data only as long as your account is connected
  • When you disconnect your account, we delete all associated data within 30 days
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information

5. Data Sharing

PomesTeam does NOT sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. We only share data with:

  • Social media platforms (TikTok, Instagram, Facebook) as necessary to provide our services
  • Service providers who assist in operating PomesTeam (hosting, analytics), under strict confidentiality agreements

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Revoke PomesTeam's access to your social media accounts at any time
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Opt out of any non-essential data collection

7. Children's Privacy

PomesTeam is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the PomesTeam platform.
