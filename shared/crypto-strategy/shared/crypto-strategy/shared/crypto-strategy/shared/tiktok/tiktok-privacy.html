PomesTeam — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how PomesTeam ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the PomesTeam application (the "App") that integrates with TikTok's API.

1. Information We Collect

PomesTeam does not collect, store, or process any personal data from end users. The App is used solely by the PomesTeam account owner to manage and publish pet-related content on TikTok.

2. How We Use TikTok Data

PomesTeam accesses the TikTok API exclusively to:

  • Publish video and photo content to the official PomesTeam TikTok account
  • Manage and schedule posts for the PomesTeam account

We do not access, collect, or store data from any other TikTok users.

3. Data Storage

PomesTeam does not maintain any database of personal information. Authentication tokens required for TikTok API access are stored securely and used only for content publishing purposes.

4. Data Sharing

PomesTeam does not sell, trade, or share any data with third parties.

5. Children's Privacy

PomesTeam does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact PomesTeam at: pomesteam@gmail.com
