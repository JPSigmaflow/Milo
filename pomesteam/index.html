How It Works

PomesTeam integrates with social media platforms via their official APIs to provide content management capabilities:

  • Content Publishing (TikTok, Instagram, Facebook): Upload and publish photos, videos, and stories directly through the platform. The app uses TikTok's Content Posting API to publish video content on behalf of authorized users.
  • Analytics & Insights: Retrieve engagement data, follower demographics, and content performance metrics to help creators understand their audience.
  • Comment Management: Read and respond to comments across platforms to maintain active community engagement.
  • Multi-Platform Scheduling: Plan content across TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook from a unified dashboard.

TikTok Integration

PomesTeam uses the following TikTok API products:

  • Content Posting API: Allows authorized users to publish video content directly to their TikTok accounts. Users authenticate via TikTok Login and grant permission to post on their behalf.
  • Login Kit: Enables secure authentication so users can connect their TikTok accounts to PomesTeam for content management.

Users can revoke access at any time through their TikTok account settings. PomesTeam only accesses data that users explicitly authorize.
